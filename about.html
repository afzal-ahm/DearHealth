About Us

Prarthana Goyal

Clinical Dietitian, Diabetes Educator & PCOD Expert

Dietitian Prarthana is a Clinical Dietitian, Diabetes Educator, Hormonal Health Coach and a PCOD Expert. She holds a Bachelor's and a Master's Degree in Food and Nutrition along with various certifications in fields like Ayurvedic Diet and Nutrition, Cardiac Nutrition, Science of Exercise, Weight Management etc. She has worked with some renowned hospitals like Shree Aggarsain International Hospital, Guru Gobind Singh Government Hospital, Columbia Asia Hospital and various Endocrinology Care Centres for Diabetes, Thyroid and PCOD.

Prarthana is passionate about optimum nutrition and believes that food is the best healer if eaten wisely but can be equally damaging if not. She also believes that you do not need a silver fork to eat good food, hence her diet plans are completely based on simple and easy home-cooked meals instead of any supplements, shakes etc. She is a keen believer of holistic and sustainable way of health and aims to make her client's health journey easier.

Dear Health Diet Clinic is a dedicated nutrition clinic and offers personalized diet consultations both in-clinic and online. All our diet plans are designed to support long-term health. Online consultations offer the same level of personalized care and follow-up as in-person visits. Flexible consultation options ensure continuity of care, no matter where you are.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to take control of their health through evidence-based nutrition education and personalized dietary interventions. We believe that proper nutrition is the foundation of a healthy life, and our mission is to make quality nutritional care accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To create a healthier society where every individual has access to personalized nutrition guidance and the knowledge to make informed dietary choices. We envision a world where preventable chronic diseases are minimized through proper nutrition and lifestyle modifications.

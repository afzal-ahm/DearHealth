Nutrition During Pregnancy and Lactation

Pregnancy: Pregnancy is the most nutritionally demanding time for a women's body where her nourishment directly affects her baby's health, growth, and development. At DearHealth, we keep in mind about the growing need of nutrition by one's body as well as the cravings associated with the hormonal changes. Join us and let us help you make this beautiful journey smooth and healthy!

Lactation: Increased nutritional demand of mothers does not end after pregnancy. The lactation phase is also of vital importance especially during the first few months of a baby's life as all his nutrients are derived from it. A healthy mother can feed and care for her baby better than an unhealthy one.

Hence, join us at DearHealth to regain your health back without affecting your milk production.
